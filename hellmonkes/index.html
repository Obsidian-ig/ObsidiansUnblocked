Welcome to HellMonkes, a thrilling blend of gorilla tag fan game with elements of horror and soon-to-be added FPS features! Dive into the chilling atmosphere of abandoned facilities and worlds overrun by eerie creatures as you embark on missions to unlock powerful weapons, unique cosmetics, and valuable in-game items. In HellMonkes, you find yourself navigating through desolate environments filled with lurking dangers and unknown horrors. Whether it's a derelict research facility or a haunted forest, each location promises adrenaline-pumping challenges and heart-pounding encounters. But beware, for you are not alone in these forsaken places. Sinister entities and grotesque creatures lurk in the shadows, ready to pounce at the slightest misstep. Your survival instincts and quick reflexes will be put to the ultimate test as you navigate through these treacherous environments. In addition to weapons, you can also unlock a bunch of cosmetics to customize your character, allowing you to stand out in the darkness or blend seamlessly with the shadows. Whether it's a menacing mask or a sleek outfit, your appearance can be as intimidating or inconspicuous as you desire.
