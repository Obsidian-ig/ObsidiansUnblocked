Game Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to our game and governs how we collect, use, and disclose information collected from users.

Data Collection

Our game collects the following data:

  • Nothing - Our game does not collect any data from the user

Use of Microphone

Our game may request access to your device's microphone for certain features. However, the microphone is only used when explicitly allowed by the user, and it is not accessed when the app is not running.

Use of IP Address

In certain cases, such as for enforcing bans, our game may check your IP address, but does not store it anywhere.

Data Usage

We do not collect any data from the user. We use the microphone in order for the player to communicate with other players in game, we do not store any voice recordings. The game may check the players ip address to make sure they are not banned from the game, but we DO NOT store any ip addresses.

Data Deletion

Because our game, and service do not collect any data, it can not be requested for deletion.
