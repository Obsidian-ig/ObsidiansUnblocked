Game Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to our game and governs how we collect, use, and disclose information collected from users.

Data Collection

Our game collects the following data:

  • Username
  • In-game ID

Use of Microphone

Our game may request access to your device's microphone for certain features. However, the microphone is only used when explicitly allowed by the user, and it is not accessed when the app is not running.

Use of IP Address

In certain cases, such as for enforcing bans, our game may collect and store your IP address.

Data Usage

We do not collect any data beyond the username, in-game ID, and IP address (if necessary for ban enforcement). We do not share or sell this data to third parties.

Data Deletion

Users cannot delete the data collected by our game. This data is used for game functionality and may be stored for as long as necessary for legal or operational purposes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at villocityontiktok@gmail.com.
